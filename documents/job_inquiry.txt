
Job Openings and Application Process:

1. Current Openings:
   - Visit careers.company.com for latest openings.
   - Open roles are listed with job descriptions, qualifications, and location.

2. Application Process:
   - Submit resume and cover letter through the portal.
   - You will receive an automated email confirmation.
   - Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for interviews.

3. Internships:
   - Summer and Winter internships are open to students in final year.
   - Apply through the same careers portal.

4. Referral Program:
   - Existing employees can refer candidates internally.
   - Referrals must be submitted before the candidate applies.

For questions, contact hr-careers@company.com.
